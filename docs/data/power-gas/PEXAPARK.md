============================================================

Pexapark

## Overview

![](/img/data/pexapark.jpg)

Pexapark AG is headquartered in Schlieren, Switzerland.They are the major third-party provider of price intelligence and risk management software specifically for the renewable energy and BESS (Battery Energy Storage Systems) markets.They provide market-based data for Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs), Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS), and renewable energy price benchmarks.

For more information, visit: https://pexapark.com/
