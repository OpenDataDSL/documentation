Baringa

## Overview

![](/img/data/baringa.png)

Baringa is a London-headquartered, independent management consulting firm founded in 2000, renowned for its people-first culture and expertise in energy, financial services, and sustainability. With over 1,300 employees and global hubs, Baringa helps companies and governments navigate complex transformations, decarbonisation, and digital strategy.

For more information, visit: https://www.baringa.com/en/
