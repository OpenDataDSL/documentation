AEM
============================================================

Arrow Energy Markets

## Overview

![](/img/data/aem.jpg)

Arrow Energy Markets (AEM) focus on Over the Counter (OTC) broking of swaps and forwards of crude oil and refined products. On exchange we cover WTI, Brent, Dubai Crude, Gasoil and heating oil futures contracts. Daily volumes in excess of 40 million barrels in 2025 to date.

The business was established in May 2024, providing specialised broking services to our customers. Our client base consists of major oil companies, independent refiners, producers, governments, banks, traders and investment funds.

For more information, visit: website: https://www.aemarkets.com/
